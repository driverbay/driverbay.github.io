Download The Latest Printer Drivers

One of the most important things to do when you set up a new printer is to download the latest drivers. Drivers are what enable your computer to communicate with your printer and they are usually available for free on the manufacturer’s website.

If you can’t find the drivers you need on the manufacturer’s website, there are a few other places you can look. DriverGuide.com is a comprehensive database of device drivers, and it’s likely that they have the driver you need. You can also try using a driver update tool like DriverMax or Driver Genius to scan your system for outdated drivers and install the latest ones automatically.
